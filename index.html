Certification and creation plane

Agent turns services into certified operational state.

Layer 8 Agent lists monitored services, evaluates health, tracks uptime, exposes MCP connections, and uses AI to build new service integrations from plain English.

Apps and incidents. Internet / Edge, WAN / SD-WAN, DC / Core, LAN / WLAN, and Firewall / RA domains roll up status while incidents stay visible.
AI-guided creation. Describe a device, service, or platform. Layer 8 researches the integration, selects credentials, and prepares MCP-backed monitoring.
Health rules. AI-selected metrics, thresholds, degraded states, and down states turn telemetry into certification logic.
Runtime visibility. Every app and MCP service is containerized, visible, restartable, and measurable from the Control Panel.
